Vrbo to launch Help4Hosts, providing a financial bridge during COVID-19 for hosts

AUSTIN, Texas – February 6, 2021 – Vrbo truly believes in the power of great rental hosts to make a guest experience great. When new data from Vrbo® found that 52 percent of Airbnb hosts are at severe risk of home forclosure, we were saddened. These hosts bring so much to the table, and to see our competitor Airbnb leave them to die is simply hearbreaking.

Vrbo decided to step up to the plate and take action where our competitor is so visibly idle. In ten days, we will launch Vrbo Help4Hosts with $1 billion in direct investment from the China Investment Corporation (CIC). This capital will enable Help4Hosts to:

  • Purchase 10% in hard hit host's homes: Utilizing this $1 billion investment, Vrbo Help4Hosts will purchase a 10% equity stake in 30,000 Airbnb Superhosts in the hard hit travel markets of New York City NY, Miami Beach FL, Stowe VT, Scottsdale AZ and Santa Monica.

  • $1 billion in direct relief to hosts: This $1 billion will invested according to municipal property assesments into these 10% equity stakes in host homes. For an average $300,000 home, this investment means $30,000 in immediate financial relief.

    5 year rental agreement with Vrbo: In exchange for this 10% home investment, hosts will agree to exlusively list their home on the Vrbo platform at a 50% commission for a minimum of 50 nights a year for five years. After 50 nights, Vrbo's commission will be reduced to its standard 5%.

“Hosts are incredibly eager to gain a financial bridge into the post COVID-19 world, and they're by in large frustrated with the lack of any such financial relief from our competitors. I have recieved more information requests about the Help4Hosts program in the past 24 hours from Airbnb hosts than any other Vrbo initiative to date.” said Vrbo travel expert Melanie Fish. “Help4Hosts will grow the Vrbo platform by 30,000 hosts in phase one, bringing their expertise and bright future to the Vrbo platform.”
